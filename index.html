Spinach Artichoke Dip

The first time, I tried Spinach Artichoke Dip was at Applebee's. I felt in love with it, so I decided to recreate my own.

Photo Credit: Dish Maps

Ingredients

  • 2 cups paramesan cheese
  • 1 (10 oz) box spinach (frozen chopped, thawed and drained)
  • 1 (14 oz) Artichoke hearts
  • 2/3 cups sour cream
  • 1 cup cream cheese
  • 2 teaspoons garlic (minced)

Making the Dip

  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Mix together paramesan cheese, spinach, and artihoke hearts
  3. Combine remaining ingredients nad mix with spinach mixture
  4. Bake for 20-30 mins
  5. Serve with crackers or toasted bread
